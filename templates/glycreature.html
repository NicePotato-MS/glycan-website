how it works

  • gene system: every creature has editable genes that control its appearance and behavior.
  • scramble genes: click the dna button to randomize all genes and create a new creature.
  • gene editor: manually edit any gene to customize your creature exactly how you want.
  • drag & drop: drag creatures from your collection to breeding slots or the trash can.
  • breeding: combine two creatures to create offspring with mixed traits and mutations.
